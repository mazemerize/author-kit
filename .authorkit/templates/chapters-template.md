## Drafting Strategy

### Sequential Drafting (Recommended for first books)

1. Plan and draft chapters in order (CH01 -> CH02 -> CH03...)
2. Review each chapter before moving to the next
3. After completing a part, do a part-level review
4. Final full-manuscript analysis after all chapters drafted

### Key Scene First (For experienced writers)

1. Draft the most critical/exciting chapters first
2. Fill in connecting chapters
3. Ensures the emotional peaks are strong before building bridges

### Part-by-Part

1. Complete all chapters in Part 1 before starting Part 2
2. Allows for part-level revision before continuing
3. Good for non-fiction where later parts build on earlier ones
